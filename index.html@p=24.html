About us

Step-By-Step Montessori School

At Step-By-Step Montessori School, we pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence in education. With a team of passionate and dedicated educators, we strive to empower our students to reach their full potential and become lifelong learners. Our holistic approach to education focuses on the development of the whole child, nurturing their intellectual, emotional, and social growth.

As a leading institution in Accra, Ghana, Step-By-Step Montessori School offers a comprehensive and rigorous curriculum that is designed to challenge and inspire students. Our Montessori-based approach to learning emphasizes hands-on, experiential education, allowing students to explore and discover the world around them at their own pace.

In addition to academic excellence, Step-By-Step Montessori School places a strong emphasis on character development and values education. We believe in instilling in our students the importance of integrity, respect, and empathy, preparing them to become responsible and compassionate members of society.

With a supportive learning environment, Step-By-Step Montessori School provides students with the tools and resources they need to succeed. From spacious classrooms to well-equipped laboratories and outdoor recreational areas, we create an atmosphere that inspires curiosity and fosters growth in every child.

As you explore our website and learn more about Step-By-Step Montessori School, we invite you to join us on our journey of excellence in education. Together, we can empower the next generation of leaders and change-makers to make a positive impact on our world. 

Our Mission, vision and motto

Our Mission

Nurturing students' academic, social, and emotional growth, fostering a love of learning and empowering them to become independent thinkers and responsible global citizens.
