Junior High

Junior High School

Step-By-Step Montessori School's Junior High Department is committed to nurturing students during their formative years while preparing them for academic excellence. Our comprehensive curriculum is designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE), a critical milestone on their educational journey. Through a blend of traditional teaching methods and innovative approaches, our dedicated faculty members provide personalized support to guide students towards success in the West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASSCE). Beyond academics, we foster a supportive environment where students develop essential life skills, critical thinking abilities, and a strong sense of social responsibility. With a focus on holistic growth, we empower students to become confident, well-rounded individuals prepared to navigate the challenges of the future with confidence and resilience.

Hands-On Approach

Students are encouraged to take a hands-on approach to learning. Through practical activities, experiments, and real-world projects, students actively engage with the curriculum, gaining a deeper understanding of concepts and developing problem-solving skills. This experiential learning approach not only enhances academic achievement but also fosters creativity, curiosity, and a love for learning. By applying their knowledge in practical contexts, students are better prepared to tackle challenges and succeed in their academic endeavors.

Nuturing Enviroment

In our Junior High Department, we prioritize creating a nurturing environment where students thrive academically and personally. With small class sizes and dedicated educators, we foster meaningful connections, encourage curiosity, and provide personalized learning experiences. Through mentorship programs and extracurricular activities, we empower students to develop their talents, build relationships, and embrace challenges with confidence. Our aim is to inspire students to reach their full potential and become compassionate leaders in their communities.
